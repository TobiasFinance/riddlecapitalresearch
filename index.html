About Us

We are a student-led club that started in 2025 to provide finance students at KEDGE with an opportunity to gain real-world experience in financial research.

Our goal is to explore different markets, conduct in-depth financial analysis, and develop investment strategies that prepare members for careers in finance.

Please feel free to reach out to us for more information.
